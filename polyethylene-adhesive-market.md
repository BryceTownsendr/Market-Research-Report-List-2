<p><p>The polyethylene adhesive market consists of four types: water-based adhesive, solvent-based adhesive, hot melt adhesive, and pressure-sensitive adhesive. Water-based adhesive is made from water as the primary solvent and is environmentally friendly. Solvent-based adhesive uses a solvent as the primary solution and offers strong bonding properties. Hot melt adhesive is heated before application and then solidifies quickly, providing a strong bond. Pressure-sensitive adhesive adheres to surfaces upon application of slight pressure and is commonly used in tapes and labels.</p></p>
