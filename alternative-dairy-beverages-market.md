Coconut milk is made by blending coconut meat and water, while rice milk is made from milled rice and water. Oat milk is made from oats blended with water. Other alternative dairy beverages include hemp milk, cashew milk, and pea milk, which are made from their respective sources blended with water. These beverages are popular amongst individuals with dietary restrictions or those seeking plant-based alternatives.</p></p>
