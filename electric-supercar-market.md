<p><p>The electric supercar market consists of three main types: Battery Electric Vehicles (BEV), Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEV), and Hybrid Electric Vehicles (HEV). BEVs are fully electric cars that rely solely on an electric battery for power, offering zero emissions. PHEVs combine an electric battery with a combustion engine, allowing for both electric and gasoline-powered driving. HEVs, on the other hand, use both electric and gasoline power simultaneously, with the electric battery assisting the combustion engine to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions.</p></p>
