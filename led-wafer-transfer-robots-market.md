<p><p>LED wafer transfer robots are used in the semiconductor industry to transfer LED wafers during the manufacturing process. There are two main types of these robots: atmospheric wafer transfer robots and vacuum wafer transfer robots. Atmospheric wafer transfer robots use atmospheric pressure to grip and transfer the wafers, while vacuum wafer transfer robots use a vacuum suction mechanism. Both types of robots are essential for efficient and precise handling of LED wafers, ensuring accurate positioning and reducing the risk of damage or contamination during the transfer process.</p></p>
