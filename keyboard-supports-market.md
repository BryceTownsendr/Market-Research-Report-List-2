<p><p>Keyboard supports are accessories designed to enhance ergonomics while using a computer keyboard. The rise in the prevalence of RSIs and other musculoskeletal disorders among office workers has led to the growing demand for keyboard supports. Furthermore, the shift towards remote working and the increasing number of home offices have also contributed to market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the keyboard supports market is the integration of advanced technologies. Manufacturers are incorporating features such as adjustable height, tilt, and swivel options to provide customizable support to users. Additionally, there is a growing focus on using sustainable materials in the production of keyboard supports to cater to the rising demand for eco-friendly products.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the increasing adoption of compact and portable keyboard supports.
