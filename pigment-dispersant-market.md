<p><p>The pigment dispersant market offers various types of dispersants that aid in the even distribution of pigments in paint, ink, and coating industries. Nonionic dispersants are versatile and widely used due to their compatibility with different pigments. Cationic dispersants are effective in dispersing pigments with negative charges. Anionic dispersants are suitable for dispersing pigments with positive charges. Lastly, amphoteric dispersants possess both positive and negative charges, enabling them to disperse a wide range of pigment types. These different types cater to diverse requirements in the pigment dispersant market.</p></p>
