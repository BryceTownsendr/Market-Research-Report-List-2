<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Industrial Smart Camera market can be classified into two types: Area Scan Camera and Line Scan Camera. The Area Scan Camera captures an image with a plane of sensors, allowing it to capture a complete image of an object. It is suitable for applications requiring detailed inspection or identification of objects in a fixed field of view. Conversely, the Line Scan Camera captures an image line by line, making it ideal for inspecting continuously moving objects such as conveyor belts, where a single line of sensors can capture multiple images.</p></p>
