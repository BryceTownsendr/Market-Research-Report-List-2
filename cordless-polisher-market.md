<p><p>Cordless polishers are an essential tool in the automotive and detailing industry. There are two main types of cordless polishers: rotary cordless polishers and dual action cordless polishers. </p><p>Rotary cordless polishers provide a high-speed, single rotation polishing motion. They are ideal for removing heavy scratches or swirl marks but require more skill to avoid damage to the paintwork.</p><p>Dual action cordless polishers, on the other hand, combine both rotary and random orbital movements simultaneously. This makes them safer and more beginner-friendly, as they reduce the risk of paint damage while still delivering excellent polishing results.</p><p>Both types offer the advantage of cordless operation, providing freedom of movement without the hassle of a power cord.</p></p>
