<p><p>Walk-through metal detectors are security devices used to detect metallic objects on a person's body. They are categorized into single zone and multi-zone types based on their detection capabilities. Single zone detectors have only one detection area, which is appropriate for small spaces and low-security environments. On the other hand, multi-zone detectors have multiple detection areas, allowing for greater precision in pinpointing the location of the detected metal objects. These are suitable for high-security areas such as airports and government buildings, where a higher level of accuracy and sophistication is required.</p></p>
