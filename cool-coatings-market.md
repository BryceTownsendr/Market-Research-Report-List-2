There are different types of cool coatings available in the market. Acrylic cool coatings are made from acrylic resins and provide good adhesion to various substrates. Silicone cool coatings are made from silicone polymers and offer excellent resistance to weathering and UV rays. Aluminum cool coatings contain aluminum flakes and provide high reflectivity. Other types of cool coatings may include elastomeric coatings or ceramic coatings, among others.</p></p>
