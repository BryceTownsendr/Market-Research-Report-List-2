This type of pitcher claims to offer health benefits by neutralizing acidity in the body and providing antioxidant properties.</p><p>The "others" market category includes pitchers with different filtration technologies, such as ceramic filters or UV lights, that target specific contaminants or provide additional features like bacteria or virus removal.</p></p>
