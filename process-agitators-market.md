<p><p>Process agitators are widely used in various industries to mix, blend, and stir liquids or viscous materials in tanks, reactors, and other containers. The market for process agitators can be categorized into different types. Top-entry agitators are mounted from the top of a tank, offering easy access for maintenance and cleaning. Side-entry agitators are installed on the side of a tank and are suitable for large vessels. Bottom-entry agitators are positioned at the bottom of a tank for efficient mixing. Portable agitators are movable, allowing flexibility in different applications. Static agitators, on the other hand, remain stationary and are ideal for continuous stirring processes.</p></p>
