<p><p>Flow wrap machines are commonly used in packaging applications across various industries. There are two main types of flow wrap machines, namely horizontal and vertical.</p><p>Horizontal flow wrap machines are designed to package products that are long and flat, such as chocolate bars or cookies. These machines horizontally feed and wrap the product in a continuous motion, ensuring efficient and smooth packaging.</p><p>On the other hand, vertical flow wrap machines are suitable for packaging products that are irregularly shaped or have varying sizes, like soaps or hardware items. These machines package products vertically, allowing flexibility to accommodate different product dimensions.</p><p>Both horizontal and vertical flow wrap machines offer automated and high-speed packaging solutions, catering to different product requirements and enhancing overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
