<p><p>The single layer PCB market comprises three types, namely single layer rigid PCB, single layer flexible PCB, and single layer rigid-flex PCB. Single layer rigid PCB refers to a type of circuit board with a solid structure, commonly used in electronic devices. Single layer flexible PCB, on the other hand, possesses an ability to bend and twist, making it suitable for applications where flexibility is required. Lastly, single layer rigid-flex PCB combines the features of both rigid and flexible PCBs, offering a versatile solution for various electronic applications.</p></p>
