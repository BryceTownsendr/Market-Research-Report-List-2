<p><p>The off-highway diesel common rail injection system market includes two types of injectors - conventional solenoid injectors and piezoelectric injectors. Conventional solenoid injectors work by using an electromagnetic coil to control fuel injection timing and quantity. On the other hand, piezoelectric injectors rely on a piezoelectric crystal to create pressure for precise fuel delivery. Both types of injectors play a crucial role in the off-highway diesel market by ensuring efficient and accurate fuel injection, leading to improved engine performance and reduced emissions.</p></p>
