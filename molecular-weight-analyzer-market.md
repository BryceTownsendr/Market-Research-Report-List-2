<p><p>The Molecular Weight Analyzer market has several types, including Gel Permeation Chromatography (GPC), Gas Chromatography & Mass Spec (GC/MS), Size Exclusion Chromatography (SEC), High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC), Differential Refractive Index (DRI), and Multi-Angle Laser Light Scattering (MALLS). GPC is used to measure the molecular weight of polymers. GC/MS combines gas chromatography and mass spectrometry for analyzing compounds. SEC separates molecules based on size. HPLC separates mixtures for analysis. DRI measures the changes in refractive index to determine molecular weights. MALLS utilizes laser light to analyze macromolecules in solution.</p></p>
