These devices provide high ESD protection levels and are known for their robustness and reliability. They are commonly used in applications where high voltage and high current handling capabilities are required.</p><p>On the other hand, Semiconductor Base ESD protection devices employ semiconductor material as the base for protecting electronic components from ESD events. These devices offer low clamping voltage and fast response times, making them suitable for applications where low capacitance and high-speed data transmission are crucial.</p><p>Both Ceramic Base and Semiconductor Base ESD protection devices play a vital role in safeguarding electronic components from damaging electrostatic discharges.</p></p>
