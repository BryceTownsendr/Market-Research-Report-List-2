<p><ul><li>Platform-based</li><li>Instrument-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cancer diagnostics market can be categorized into platform-based and instrument-based types. In platform-based diagnostics, the focus is on the use of specific platforms or technologies for detecting and diagnosing cancer, such as gene expression profiling, next-generation sequencing, or immunoassays. On the other hand, instrument-based diagnostics involve the use of specific instruments or devices that aid in the detection and analysis of cancer, for example, imaging techniques like MRI or CT scans. These two types encompass different approaches for diagnosing cancer, providing healthcare professionals with a range of tools to identify and monitor the disease.</p></p>
