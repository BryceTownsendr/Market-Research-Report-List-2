<p><p>Lens antennas are a type of antenna that use a dielectric lens to shape and focus the radio waves. Two common types are horn lens antennas and Luneberg lens antennas. Horn lens antennas have a horn-shaped structure that guides the radio waves, allowing for high directivity and gain. Luneberg lens antennas, on the other hand, use a spherical lens with varying permittivity to achieve a wide-angle beam steering capability. Both types find applications in radar systems, satellite communications, and wireless networks.</p></p>
