<p><p>The charging pile market consists of two main types: AC charging pile and DC charging pile. AC charging piles are designed to convert the alternating current from the power supply into the direct current needed to charge electric vehicles (EVs). They are commonly used for charging in residential areas and public locations. On the other hand, DC charging piles are capable of providing direct current power to EVs, allowing for faster charging times. These are commonly found in fast-charging stations along highways or in commercial areas. Both types play a crucial role in supporting the growing demand for EV charging infrastructure.</p></p>
