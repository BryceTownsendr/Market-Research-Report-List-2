<p><p>Micropower operational amplifiers are electronic devices used in various applications requiring low power consumption. They are commonly used in battery-operated devices where limited power supply is available.</p><p>On the other hand, the dual power supply type of micropower operational amplifiers require both positive and negative power supplies to function. They are often used in applications that demand wider input and output voltage ranges where a balance of positive and negative voltage is necessary for operation.</p></p>
