<p><p>The ultra-thin stainless steel sheets market consists of two main types: cold rolled and hot rolled. Cold rolled sheets undergo a process where they are rolled at room temperature, resulting in a smooth and durable finish. These sheets are known for their high strength and dimensional accuracy, making them suitable for various industrial applications. On the other hand, hot rolled sheets are produced by heating stainless steel above its recrystallization temperature and then rolling it. This process results in sheets with a rougher surface finish and are commonly used in structural applications.</p></p>
