<p><p>The cosmetic contact lenses market encompasses three main types: rigid contact lenses, soft contact lenses, and hybrid contact lenses. Rigid contact lenses are made from durable materials and retain their shape on the eye, providing clearer vision but potentially causing discomfort. Soft contact lenses are more flexible, offering better comfort but may not achieve as precise vision correction. Hybrid contact lenses combine the benefits of both rigid and soft lenses, having a rigid center for clear vision and a soft outer rim for improved comfort. Each type has its advantages and considerations for cosmetic use.</p></p>
