<p><p>The Distributed Fiber Optic Sensor (DFOS) market consists of three main types: Distributed Temperature Sensing (DTS), Distributed Acoustic Sensing (DAS), and Others (such as Distributed Strain Sensing - DSS). DTS is used to measure temperature changes along a fiber optic cable, enabling monitoring of pipelines and power grids. DAS, on the other hand, detects acoustic signals and is commonly employed in infrastructure monitoring and security applications. The "Others" category encompasses technologies like DSS, which measures strain and deformation in structures like bridges and dams, providing valuable insight into their integrity and safety.</p></p>
