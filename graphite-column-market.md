Bubble-cap columns feature gas-dispersing caps that break down bubbles, ensuring an efficient exchange of components. Sieve trays columns contain perforated trays that allow vapor to rise and liquid to descend, promoting effective distillation. Other types of columns may include valve trays, disc and doughnut trays, or even combinations of different tray designs. Each type offers distinct advantages and is chosen based on specific process requirements.</p></p>
