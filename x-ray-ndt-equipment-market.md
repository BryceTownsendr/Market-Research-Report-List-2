<p><p>The X-ray NDT (Non-Destructive Testing) equipment market comprises three main types - CR, DR, and CT. CR (Computed Radiography) uses a phosphor imaging plate to capture X-ray images, which are then digitally scanned for analysis. DR (Digital Radiography) directly converts X-rays into electronic signals, providing faster image acquisition and higher resolution. CT (Computed Tomography) involves rotating an X-ray source and detector around an object to generate detailed cross-sectional images. These equipment types find applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, healthcare, and manufacturing, enabling efficient and accurate inspection without damaging the tested materials.</p></p>
