<p><p>Automatic case packers are machines used in packaging processes to efficiently group and pack products in their respective cases. There are two main types of automatic case packers: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic case packers require manual intervention, such as product loading and case sealing, while the machine handles the case forming and product packing. On the other hand, fully automatic case packers require minimal human interaction as they can perform all tasks autonomously, including case forming, product loading, and case sealing. Both types offer advantages in terms of efficiency, productivity, and reduced labor costs in packaging operations.</p></p>
