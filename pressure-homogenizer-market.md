<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Pressure homogenizers are devices used to blend or emulsify substances with high pressure. </p><p>Single-valve assembly pressure homogenizers consist of a single valve, where the substance is pushed through a small orifice at high pressure to achieve homogeneity. These devices are suitable for processing low-viscosity fluids.</p><p>Two-valve assembly pressure homogenizers utilize two valves. The substance is first forced through a primary valve at high pressure and then through a secondary valve at even higher pressure. This design is suitable for processing high-viscosity fluids and achieving finer particle sizes.</p></p>
