<p><p>The Cold Work Die Steel Market includes various types of dies used in different industrial processes. Blanking dies are employed to cut out desired shapes from sheet metal. Cold upsetting dies are used to reshape metal parts by compressing them. Cold extrusion dies are utilized to form hollow or solid shapes by forcing metal through a die. Bending dies are used to bend sheet metal and other materials. Drawing dies are employed in the process of creating wire or tube shapes by pulling metal through a die.</p></p>
