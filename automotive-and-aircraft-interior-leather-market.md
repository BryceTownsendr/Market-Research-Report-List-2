<p><p>The automotive and aircraft interior leather market offers various types of leather, including pigmented leather, aniline leather, and semi-aniline leather. Pigmented leather is coated with a layer of pigment, providing durability and resistance to stains. Aniline leather is made from high-quality hides with no additional coating, retaining the natural characteristics but being less resistant to scratches and stains. Semi-aniline leather combines the benefits of both pigmented and aniline leather, as it features a light protective coating while maintaining the natural look and feel of aniline leather.</p></p>
