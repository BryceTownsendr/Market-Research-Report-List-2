<p><p>Membrane cleaning chemicals market consists of various types, including caustic, oxidants or disinfectants, acids, surfactants, and chelating agents. Caustic chemicals are used to dissolve fatty deposits and organic compounds. Oxidants or disinfectants work by destroying microorganisms and bacteria. Acids are effective in removing inorganic scales and mineral deposits. Surfactants help to reduce surface tension, allowing better cleaning. Chelating agents are used to dissolve metal ions, preventing their deposition on the membrane surface. Overall, these different types of chemicals play crucial roles in maintaining the efficiency and lifespan of membranes in various industries.</p></p>
