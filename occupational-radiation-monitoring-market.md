<p><p>The Occupational Radiation Monitoring market includes two main types of dosimeters: passive dosimeters and active dosimeters. </p><p>Passive dosimeters are small devices worn by individuals that measure and record the amount of radiation they are exposed to over a specific period of time. They do not require any external power source and are typically analyzed in a laboratory after use.</p><p>On the other hand, active dosimeters not only measure radiation levels but also provide continuous real-time monitoring. They are equipped with alarms that alert individuals when radiation levels exceed safety limits, allowing for immediate action to be taken.</p><p>Both types of dosimeters are crucial in ensuring the safety of workers in occupations where radiation exposure is a concern.</p></p>
