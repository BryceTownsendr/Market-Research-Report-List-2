<p><p>The aluminium fluoride (AlF3) market can be categorized into three types: dry AlF3, anhydrous AlF3, and wet AlF3. Dry AlF3 refers to the fine powdered form of aluminium fluoride that is often used in the production of aluminium metal. Anhydrous AlF3 is a variant that is completely free of water content and finds applications in the pharmaceutical and ceramic industries. Wet AlF3, on the other hand, is an aqueous solution of aluminium fluoride and is primarily employed in the production of aluminium smelters and glass industries.</p></p>
