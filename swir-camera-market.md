<p><p>The SWIR camera market comprises two major types: SWIR area scan cameras and SWIR line scan cameras. SWIR area scan cameras capture images in a similar way to traditional digital cameras, by taking a snapshot of a specific area. These cameras are suitable for applications that require capturing still images or moving objects. On the other hand, SWIR line scan cameras capture continuous images line by line, making them appropriate for scenarios like inspecting moving objects on a conveyor belt or scanning large areas. Both types find application in various industries such as manufacturing, defense, and healthcare.</p></p>
